Project Overview:

In efforts to study more about bacteria species that synthesize proteins to taste like beef, these data visualizations are to help undrestand more about these species. As a hypothesis, it is believed that the ideal bacterial species to make the best synthetic beef comes from the human belly button. To test this hypothesis, belly buttons were tested to identify the different bacterias in each subject's belly button. Each subject that was tested was given a unique ID number. To make the research findings easy for researchers and participants to visualize, she made this dashboard. Feel free to use the interactive charts below by selecting the users ID on the left hand side of the webpage.
